The problem

Crypto payments are final.

If the merchant doesn't deliver, your money is gone. No chargeback. No recourse.

Buyers won't transact without a way back. This is the wall between stablecoins and real commerce.

The gap · this hackathon

48 projects. One empty lane.

Everyone builds control before the payment — approvals, KYC, maker-checker, risk scanners.

Nobody gives the payer recourse after.

The solution

Escrow bound to an on-chain deliverable + deadline.

Buyer signs 1 HSP mandate → open() → [HELD] ├─ merchant delivers the exact asset → atomic DvP → merchant paid └─ deadline passes, undelivered → buyer reclaims. money kembali.

Deliver → asset to buyer, funds to merchant, atomically. Don't → the buyer's money comes back.
